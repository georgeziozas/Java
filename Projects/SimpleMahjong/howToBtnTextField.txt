This is a Western version of the popural mahjong solitaire puzzle game.

This version of mahjong consistis of a range of Tiles placed in a board.

The Tiles in this version are placed in one level only , meaning that a tile wont be on Top of another.
The game is finsihed when the player sucesfully erases all the pairs of Tiles OR when its not possible to erase any of the one that are left.
Tiles:.

Characters : 9 character Tiles * 4 rows of them.
Baboo : 9 baboo Tiles * 4 rows of them.
Circle : 9 baboo Tiles * 4 rows of them.
Seasons: 4 Tiles * 2 rows of them.
Flowers: 4 Tiles * 2 rows of them.
Rules:
A player can delete a pair of Tiles only when these rules are followed:.
each Tile is free: Tile must not have a neighbour at left OR right.
The pair is valid:.
	-Character,baboo,Circle (when the tile is in one of this 3 it can be mixed only with exactly the same Tile).
	ex: lets say i choose a character Tile with a specific name: 1x then it can be erased only with 1x Tile of character.

	-Seasons,Flowers (when the tile is one of these 2 then it can be matched with each other).
	ex: 1 flower can be matched with any flower and also with any season.
here are the bonus functions you can use:.
-reshuffle the board.
-Get a guess of the next move.
-undo - redo Move.
-how many tiles are free at the current time.
